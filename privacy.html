Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

In short: Your PDF files are processed entirely inside your own browser. They are never uploaded to, transmitted to, or stored on any server. The data described below relates only to advertising and standard website hosting.

1. Who We Are

Free PDF Merger ("we," "us," or "the Service") operates the website freepdfmergeonline.netlify.app, a free tool that lets you combine multiple PDF files into one. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the Service and how it is handled.

2. Your PDF Files Are Not Collected

The merging process runs locally in your web browser using client-side technology (the pdf-lib library). This means:

  • Your PDF files never leave your device.
  • We do not upload, receive, view, store, or retain any documents you process.
  • Once you close or reload the page, any files held in memory are discarded.

Because of this design, we have no access to the contents of your documents at any point.

3. Information Collected Automatically

While the tool itself does not collect your files, some standard information is collected automatically when you visit the site, primarily to display advertising and to operate and secure the website:

  • Advertising data — through Google AdSense (see Section 4), including cookies and similar identifiers, your approximate location, device and browser information, and how you interact with ads.
  • Technical and log data — our hosting provider may automatically record information such as your IP address, browser type, and access times as part of normal server operation and security.

No method of transmission or processing is ever completely secure, but our client-side design keeps your file data on your own device.
